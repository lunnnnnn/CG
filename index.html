Over the centuries, lanterns have become a staple item in China, especially during celebrations.
Different types of Chinese lanterns exist for many occasions and each type has its own unique meaning.
There are different forms of lanterns, which include hanging lanterns, sky lanterns, and water lanterns.
We chose to render hanging lanterns inculding the main body as well as the rope it was attached.
